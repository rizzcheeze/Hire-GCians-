About the platform

A campus hiring app built around fit, not just forms.

Hire GCians! is designed to connect Gordon College students with third-party employers using structured profiles, job requirements, and AI-assisted resume analysis.

Core ideas
What the platform is trying to solve
Better student matching
Students should see openings ranked by skill fit instead of manually checking every posting.
Cleaner employer review
Third-party employers need one place to publish roles, screen applicants, and move strong candidates forward.
Resume intelligence
The planned AI pipeline should scan PDF resumes, identify skills, and detect whether achievements are measurable or quantifiable.
